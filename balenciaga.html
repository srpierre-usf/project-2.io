BALENCIAGA

FALL '24

The collection, designed by Demna Gvasalia, explores the intersection of couture heritage and modern street culture under the idea of “creativity as the new luxury.” Presented outdoors in Los Angeles along a palm-lined street, the show captured the city’s mix of casual athleticism and red-carpet glamour. The collection moved from minimalist activewear and Y2K-inspired tracksuits to sharp, sculptural tailoring and dramatic evening gowns that nodded to Cristóbal Balenciaga’s classic silhouettes. Standout items included the oversized alask boots and Erewhon branded paper bags.
