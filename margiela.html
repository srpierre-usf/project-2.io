MAISON MARGIELA

ARTISNAL '24

Designed by John Galliano, this collection transformed the ritual of dressing into a theatrical study of identity and emotion. Staged beneath Paris’s Pont Alexandre III on a rain-soaked night, the show unfolded like a gothic dreamscape, merging couture precision with cinematic storytelling.Galliano reimagined the body as a canvas, shaping silhouettes through corsetry and bias-cut tailoring that blurred vulnerability and strength. The collection’s most notable pieces included sculpted hourglass gowns and deconstructed suits rendered with techniques that produce a haunting yet romantic exploration of craftsmanship, where haute couture met the poetic grit of Paris’s nocturnal underworld.
